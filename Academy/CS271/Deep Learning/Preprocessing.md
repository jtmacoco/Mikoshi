## Feature Scaling
- Ensure that the numbers become small enough
- And that the relationships among different samples and different features will be maintained
- So if something is bigger it will still be bigger
- Formula: $\frac{x-\mu}{\sigma}$
- Feature scaling helps ensure that the numbers become small enough and the relation ships among different features and samples will be maintained
- This is similar to z-score in statistics
- So applying the feature scaling makes the values more manageable.
- Want numbers to be balanced  not to big or to small
