>[!important] Embedding 
> An embedding is a learned numerical representation of something (like text, images, or code) that places similar things close together in high-dimensional space

**Dimensions** are like columns (features)
Example:

| item  | d1    | d2    | d3   | d4    | d5    |
| ----- | ----- | ----- | ---- | ----- | ----- |
| "cat" | 0.12  | -0.44 | 0.91 | 0.03  | -0.77 |
| "dog" | 0.10  | -0.40 | 0.88 | 0.02  | -0.74 |
| "car" | -0.92 | 0.33  | 0.05 | -0.80 | 0.21  |
In this embedding dimension = 5
